Nature Awaits You!

The city of Bozeman

Come to Bozeman, your base camp for a family friendly and action packed Montana getaway. All of the National Forest Campgrounds on the Bozeman District are reservable with the exception of Spanish Creek, Battleridge, and Fairy Lake which are first come first served.

Adventure

Bozeman is home for the best adventure you can have. Just ask around, or do a little research, or simply come and see for yourself. Bozeman Yellowstone International Airport is the gateway to spectacular views.

Biking

Do you enjoy biking? Looking to have the best experience of your life? Come to Bozeman mountain bike trail and experience the ultimate ride. Rent a bicycle if needed and get route recommendations in town.
